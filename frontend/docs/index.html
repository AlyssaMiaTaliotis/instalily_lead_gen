A
Data Ingestion & Normalization
Raw data from multiple sources is ingested, cleaned, and normalized. Company names are standardized, duplicate entries are identified and merged, and data quality scores are assigned to each record.
B
ICP Matching & Scoring
AI evaluates each company against DuPont Tedlar's ideal customer profile. Scoring considers industry vertical, company size, technology needs, geographic presence, and growth trajectory. Companies scoring >80% are flagged as high-priority leads.
C
Stakeholder Intelligence
For qualified companies, the system identifies key decision-makers using LinkedIn Sales Navigator API integration. Contacts are ranked by influence, decision-making authority, and accessibility for outreach.
D
Output Generation
Processed data is formatted into structured outputs including company profiles, contact information, qualification rationale, and personalized outreach messages. Data is exported to CRM-ready formats with full audit trails.
